Privacy Policy

1. Overview

FutureJob (https://www.futurejob.site) is a free AI-based future career recommendation service. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • User-provided inputs: Name (optional), birth year, country, education level, RIASEC type, Big5 personality type
  • Usage data: Pages visited, buttons clicked, country and language selection (via Google Analytics 4)
  • Device data: Browser type, operating system, screen size (collected automatically by analytics tools)

We do not collect:

  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Payment or financial information
  • Precise GPS location
  • Any data from users under the age of 13

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized future career recommendations
  • To improve the service based on usage patterns
  • To measure the reach and performance of the service (analytics only)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics 4 — usage analytics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudflare Pages — website hosting (Cloudflare Privacy Policy)
  • Kakao SDK — social sharing for Korean users (Kakao Privacy Policy)

5. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies through Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our service. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

6. Data Retention

User-entered form data (name, birth year, etc.) is processed in-session only and is not stored on our servers. Analytics data is retained for up to 26 months in accordance with Google Analytics default settings.

7. Children's Privacy

FutureJob is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking (use browser ad-block or Google Analytics opt-out)
